Privacy Policy for Ririko AI (Ririko Bot)

Last updated: July 1st 2023

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Emails: We may collect email addresses for the purpose of authentication, sending email notifications, and marketing purposes. For marketing emails, users can always unsubscribe and remove themselves from all email marketing listings.

1.2 Discord Server Names and Number of Members: We collect Discord server names and the number of members for statistical purposes. This information helps us understand the usage and popularity of Ririko AI.

1.3 Number of API Calls: For certain functions, we collect the number of API calls made to Ririko AI. This information is collected for monitoring and improving the delivery of our services only.

1.4 Conversation with the Chat Bot (OpenAI and NLPCloud): We collect and store conversation data with the Chat Bot, powered by OpenAI and NLPCloud, for the purpose of better prompting and recalling past conversations. It allows us to improve the chat experience. Please note that we do not monitor any chat conversations that are not related to the chat bot service.

2. Use of Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

2.1 Authentication and Communication: We use email addresses to authenticate users and facilitate communication, including sending email notifications related to Ririko AI.

2.2 Statistical Analysis: The Discord server names and number of members are used for statistical analysis to understand usage patterns and improve the functionality of Ririko AI.

2.3 Service Improvement: We may analyze the number of API calls to monitor and improve the delivery of our services, ensuring optimal performance and reliability.

2.4 Chat Experience Enhancement: Conversation data with the Chat Bot is used to improve the prompting and recalling of past conversations, enhancing the overall chat experience.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties for marketing or any other purposes without your explicit consent. However, we may share your information in the following cases:

3.1 Service Providers: We may engage trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating and improving Ririko AI. These service providers have access to the information only to perform specific tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

3.2 Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal requests, such as subpoenas, court orders, or government regulations.

4. Data Retention

We retain the collected information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. If you would like us to delete your personal information, please contact us at me@angel.net.my.

5. Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

Ririko AI is not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us, and we will take steps to remove that information from our systems.
